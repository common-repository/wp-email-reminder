Add-on forWP Email Capture, sends reminder emails to the "temp-members". Everything is done from a simple menu added to the dashboard.



== Description ==

WP Email Reminder adds a menu under the settings section that enables you to send emails with confirmation links to your temp member (i.e. people who forgot to complete their registration by clicking on the link sent by [WP Email Capture](http://wpemailcapture.com/ "WP Email Capture")).

Functionalities : 

  * choice between plain text or html email
  * free selection of the recipients
  * possibility of saving email templates for later use
  * optional database cleanup


Restrictions : 

  * this plugin makes sense only if you already use WP Email Capture on your blog
  * to prevent spamming, no more than two reminders can to be send to the same recipient
 


== Installation ==

Warning : in order to use WP Email Reminder, you must have installed WP Email Capture. If you changed anything to the latter
(like the names of the tables in the database), WP Email Reminder could not work properly.

1. Upload `wp-email-reminder.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. check that WP Email Capture is installed, active and working on your site
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. enjoy :)
